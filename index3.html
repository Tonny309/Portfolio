About MCUCSA

MCUCSA is a unifying student body bringing together all students from Machakos County studying across different universities and colleges. The association promotes academic excellence, leadership, community service, and youth empowerment.

Our Mission

To empower Machakos County students through leadership, advocacy, mentorship, and impactful community engagement. We aim to build a strong network of youth who are academically and socially empowered to be future changemakers.
